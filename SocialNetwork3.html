Model

The Bayesian hierarchical model is given by:

\[ \text{logodds}(y_{i,j}=1 | Z_i, Z_j, x, \beta) = \Sigma_{k=1}^{p} x_{k,i,j}\beta_{k} - |Z_i - Z_j| \ \]

\[ \beta_k \sim N(\xi_k,\psi_{k}^2) \hspace{2mm} k = 1,\ldots,p \] \[ Z_i \sim \Sigma_{g=1}^{G} \lambda_{g}MVN_{d}(\mu_g,\sigma_{g}^2I_d) \hspace{2mm} i=1,\ldots,n \]

\[ \mu_g \sim MVN_{d}(0,\omega^2I_d) \hspace{2mm} g = 1,\ldots,G \]

\[ \sigma_{g}^2 \sim \sigma_{0}^2Inv\chi_{\alpha}^2 \hspace{2mm} g = 1,\ldots,G \] \[ (\lambda_1,\ldots,\lambda_{G}) \sim Dirichlet(\nu_1,\ldots,\nu_G) \]
